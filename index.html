3-2-1 Ribs!

3-2-1 Method

Grab a beer and get ready becuse this style of cooking is low and slow!

Ingredients

  • Pork Ribs
  • Yellow Mustard / Olive oil
  • Dry rub for ribs
  • Bbq sauce
  • Apple Juice
  • honey
  • Tin foil / Butcher paper
  • Your favorite flavored woodchips
  • Brown Sugar
  • Butter

Intstructions

  1. Remove membraine from back of ribs
  2. Coat ribs with yellow mustard or olive oil
  3. Smother ribs with your favorite dry rub
  4. Set your smoker to 225 degrees F
  5. Place your ribs bone side down for three hours
  6. In a spray bottle spritze ribs with applejuice every 30 minitues
  7. Remove ribs and wrap in tin foil or butcher paper
    and sprinkle brown sugar with some butter on top of ribs
  8. Place ribs back in smoker for two hours while wrapped
  9. Remove ribs from wrap after two hours
  10. Glaze ribs with your favorite Bbq sauce
  11. Place ribs back in smoker for one more hour
  12. Slice and enjoy
